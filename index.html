Delicious Food Places

  • Sushi + Rotary Sushi Bar
  • Qing Xiang Yuan Dumplings
  • Szechwan JMC
  • Phoenix Restaurant
  • MingHin Cuisine
  • BBQ King House

These restaurant all have different specializations. Food style from different places at China has been implemented in their cooking. From breakfast food to dinner, they have everything that you need. There are many types of Chinese dishes that are offered from Dim Sum to BBQ. Not only that, there's even a sushi place here at Chinatown.

Attractions

  • Chicago Public Library, Chinatown Branch
  • Ping Tom Memorial Park
  • Sun Yat-Sen Park
  • Armor Square Park

There's a library located right at Chinatown, so you could go borrow some books and read. Not only that, the Chinatown branch Library is pretty big and has a pretty nice appearance both internally and externally. The Ping Tom Memorial Park is near a lake, so you could go kayaking, and there's even a indoor swimming pool pretty close by. Armor Square Park also has a pool indoor, and there also places where you can play basketball both indoor and outdoor. There also many other activies at that park. Sun Yat-Sen Park has many childrens and seniors playing there, and there also a memorial for Sun Yat Sen.

Historical Sites

  • Chinatown Square
  • Chinese-American Museum of Chicago
  • Heritage Museum of Asian Art
  • Pui Tak Center
  • Nine Dragon Wall
  • Chicago Chinese Cultural Institute

These places show the history of China and Chinatown. From the 12 zodiac statues to the traditional Chinese buildings, there are many places that spread Chinese history. From a musuem filled with Chinese artifacts, arts, and such. There are also places that show the Chinese culture, and teaches people about Chinese history.
